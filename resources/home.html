Dungeon Master Suite

Instructions for Use:


Story: Type into the textbox and it will be autosaved for later use.

Event/Locations/NPCs: Click "+Add" and then fill in the relevant information. Be sure to click save. Click on an event/location/NPC to show the details for it.

Dice Roller: Use the first box to select how many dice to roll and the second box to add a modifier to the total roll.

Players: Type in the relavant boex to fill in the information for your players. Information will be saved automatically.

Misc: Right click anywhere to bring up a Quick Roll menu. Updates will not overwrite your data.
